# Limitations & Safety Constraints

## This is not legal advice

JurisSearch provides **research synthesis and decision support**.  
It does not replace attorney judgment or provide legal advice.

## Known technical limitations (general)

- **Source availability:** results depend on accessible repositories and licensing constraints
- **Jurisdiction ambiguity:** unclear scope can degrade output quality
- **Conflicting authority:** splits may require human interpretation
- **Novel fact patterns:** limited precedent can reduce confidence
- **Model limitations:** LLMs may produce plausible but incorrect statements without strict gating
