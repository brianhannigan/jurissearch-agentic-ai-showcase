# Performance Summary (Narrative)

## What “good” looks like
- Most briefings complete within a predictable time window
- High claim–citation alignment rates
- Low hallucination risk due to gating + retrieval separation
- Clear escalation when confidence drops

## Stability and reliability posture
- Orchestrator uses retry logic for transient tool failures
- Connector isolation reduces blast radius
- Observability includes traces and structured logs
- Immutable audit trails enable defensible review

This public repo is not a benchmark disclosure. It is an enterprise readiness signal.
